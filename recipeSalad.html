Instruction of My Salad

Directions


First, wash all the cucumbers,tomatoes and lettuce, chop and add to a large salad bowl as you are chopping.

Add oils, vinegar (if using), salt and pepper right on top of veggies in a bowl.


Toss gently and only just until combined. Lettuce salad tastes best fresh without fragile lettuce leaves bruised and juicy veggies leaking.


It is best to serve this lettuce salad within first 30-60 minutes after tossing. Lettuce leaves wilt really fast.
