Directions


Cook 500g of dried spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water about 7min and check the spaghetti is soft enough.

Heat oil over medium high heat,add garlic and cook until golden -be careful not to burn it.


Toss with pasta and cooking water per Base Directions.Toss through parsley and chili flakes serve with parmesan.
